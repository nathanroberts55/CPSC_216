The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?
Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt has canvas walls, wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.
How are the yurts furnished?
Each yurt is furnished with a queen-size bed with a down quilt and gas-fired stove. The luc=luxury camping experience also includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.
What should I bring?
bring a sense of adventure and some time to relax! Most guest also pack comfortable shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with layers of clothing
